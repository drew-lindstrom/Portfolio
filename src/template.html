About me

Hi and thank you for visiting my website! This website and the projects listed below were created as part of a nine month period where I worked fulltime to transition into a career in software development. While my resume is up to date, I would like to mention that this website and the projects listed below are not reflective of my current skills as a programmer. I am a fairly recent graduate from the University of Minnesota with a bachelors in Mechanical Engineering and an additional focus in Product Design. I chose those disciplines because of my love for math and design, but turns out I'm a full stack developer! While coding has always been a hobby of mine, it wasn't until fairly recently that I realized its what I'm passionate about and what I've wanted in a career all along. For over a year now, I have been working hard to hone my skills as a developer and I'm excited to show you what I've accomplished.

When I'm not busy working on programming projects, I like to spend my free time cooking, practicing guitar, weightlifting, and learning Japanese.

Projects

Pokemon Battle Game

What initially began as a simple project to practice OOP concepts quickly evolved into one of my favorite projects. This is completely self made Pokemon battle game where users can play against an AI opponent with a fully functional team. While there are currently only twelve Pokemon, this project was structured so that all the major game mechanics were included and any new Pokemon, move, or ability could easily be implemented. Although Pokemon might seem like a simple game, it has a lot more going on under the hood than what most people would expect. Currently I am working to create a better UI and implement team customization.

Personal Blog

This a blog created with Django that I use to document my daily progress in programming. Users are able to log in/create an account and can post/edit their own blog posts. It also utilizes AWS S3 for profile picture file storage, PostgreSQL for user info and blog post storage, and Heroku for deployment. For this project I followed an online course which was a great in-depth introduction to Django, AWS, and Heroku. I am currently working to add unit and functionality testing and plan to implement additional features such as adding photos to blog posts.
